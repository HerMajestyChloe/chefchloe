Fried Green Tomatoes

Chloe Nelson 07/11/2022

For my first ever post on my website, I decided to bring you all a taste of southern Ohio with my Fried Green Tomatoes! These Fried Green tomatoes are absolutely to die for with a golden crisp crunch and a mouth-watering pimento cheese.

These can be eaten as slices dipped in pimento cheese or toss them on your favorite brioche or artisan bun for a delectable sandwich filled with the taste of summer! I have been making these Fried Green Tomato for years and every year summer comes around they are the first thing I go to.

I hope you enjoy these as much as my family does and can bring a little of Southern Ohio into your home with the sweet aroma filling your kitchen!

Ingredients:

  1. 2.5 tbs of olive oil $2.04
  2. 1/3 cup of flour $2.04
  3. 1 egg $2.04
  4. 2 tbsp of milk $2.04
  5. Â½ cup of plain breadcrumbs (panko does not hold to the tomato very well, but can be used if desired)$2.04
  6. 1 tbsp of garlic powder $2.04
  7. 1 tbsp of paprika powder $2.04

    8. $2.04 Total for 12 servings!

Directions:

  1. STEP 1: Slice your green tomatoes into 4-5 even slices depending on your preference for thickness.
  2. STEP 2: Sprinkle Salt, pepper, and chili powder onto both sides of each tomato slice.
  3. STEP 3: Mix flour and garlic powder together in a small bowl.
  4. STEP 4: Mix breadcrumbs and paprika together in another small bowl.
  5. STEP 5: In another small bowl beat egg and milk together.
  6. STEP 6: Dip each tomato slice into flour, then egg, and finally breadcrumbs.
  7. STEP 7: Heat olive oil in a skillet or frying pan at medium heat.
  8. STEP 8: Add tomato slices to skillet or frying pan.
  9. STEP 9: Wait until the tomato slices start to look golden around the bottom, (about 30-45 seconds) and flip.
  10. STEP 10: Repeat step 9 for the uncooked side.
  11. STEP 11: When tomatoes are golden brown on both sides remove them and place your tomatoes on a plate with a paper towel to soak up some of the leftover oil.
  12. STEP 12: Wait until the tomato slices start to look golden around the bottom, (about 30-45 seconds) and flip.
  13. STEP 13: Serve as slices or on a brioche bun with pimento, American cheese, and shredded lettuce!
